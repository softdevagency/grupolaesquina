Our Story

Learn more about Grupo La Esquina

It all started on a corner in Huixtla, Chiapas. In 2014, La Esquina Pizza was born with authentic recipes and quality ingredients. In 2024, the same passion gave life to Ugo Sliders, a smash burger concept with 100% Angus beef, fries and milkshakes. In 2025 we brought both brands together under Grupo La Esquina S.A. de C.V. to grow as a franchise without losing our essence.

  • Quality ingredients and honest processes

  • Authentic recipes: handcrafted pizzas and 100% Angus smash burgers

  • Warm service and a focus on improving every day
