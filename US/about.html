Our Story

Learn more about Grupo La Esquina

It all started on a corner in Huixtla, Chiapas. In 2014, La Esquina Pizza was born—a small shop with a big dream: pizzas made with love, quality ingredients, and a unique recipe. In 2024, that same passion gave life to Ugo Sliders, a smash‑burger concept with 100% Angus beef, fries, and shakes. In 2025, we consolidated both brands under Grupo La Esquina S.A. de C.V. to grow as a franchise while keeping our essence.

  • Quality ingredients and honest processes

  • Artisan pizzas and 100% Angus smash burgers

  • Warm service and continuous improvement
