Data Controller

Grupo La Esquina S.A. de C.V., con domicilio en Huixtla, Chiapas, Mexico, es responsable del tratamiento de tus datos personales conforme a este Aviso de Privacy.

Personal Data We Collect

We may request: name, email, phone number, consumption preferences, payment data (through secure gateways) and reservation or order details.

Purposes of Processing

Primary: manage reservations, orders, billing, customer service and communications related to your consumption. Secondary: send promotions, satisfaction surveys and updates about our products and franchises.

Transfers

We may transfer data to service providers (e.g., payment platforms, messaging, hosting) under agreements that protect your information. We do not sell personal data.

Legal Basis and Consent

We process your data in accordance with applicable Mexican law. When necessary, we will request your consent. You may revoke it at any time using the methods indicated in this Policy.

ARCO Rights

You can exercise your rights of Access, Rectification, Cancellation and Opposition (ARCO), as well as revoke your consent, by writing to laesquinapizzas@laesquinapizzerias.com.

Security Measures

We implement reasonable administrative, technical and physical measures to protect your data against damage, loss, alteration, destruction or unauthorized use.

Use of Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies to enhance your experience and analyze site usage. You can disable them in your browser settings, which may affect some features.

Data Retention

We retain data for as long as necessary to fulfill the stated purposes and in accordance with legal or contractual requirements.

Policy Updates

We may modify this Policy at any time. The current version will always be available on this page; we recommend reviewing it periodically.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at laesquinapizzas@laesquinapizzerias.com or via WhatsApp at +52 962 471 9341.
